Introducing the Infowars Solar Base Station

Secure your energy self-sufficiency with the Infowars Solar Base Station. 

This portable solar power generator provides you with a reliable energy supply that is not dependent upon our vulnerable and deteriorating state-controlled power grid.  It comes with a high-quality 100% monocrystalline solar panel plus a specialized rolling-cart with a protective case so you take it where you need it, when you need it.  With the Infowars Solar Base Station, when the grid goes down - you stay powered up. 

We have done our research and at last found a solar generator that is deserving of the Infowars name.  The Infowars Solar Base Station is the solar generator that met all of our high standards required to earn the Alex Jones seal of approval.  

This solar generator contains a Lithium Iron Phosphate battery. This is not the usual lead-Acid based battery found in other generators that will leak, overheat, or explode. This battery is extremely stable and will last up to 8 times longer than your typical unit.

It also includes a 120 Watt monocrystalline foldable solar panel. This is not the polycrystalline panels found with other units that are cheaper and less effective. The monocrystalline panels last longer, yield higher power output, and tend to perform better than polycrystalline panels under lower light conditions.

The Infowars Solar Base Station will provide 1500 watts of continuous power and 3000 watts of peak power to meet all your emergency or camping needs. It has a built in charge controller that will ensure it will not over-charge as well as regulate the inconsistent power from the sun to give maximum battery life.

It has pure sine wave AC output and DC output, four USB outputs, 100-240 Volt AC input, as well as four Anderson plugs to connect up to four solar panels at once.
